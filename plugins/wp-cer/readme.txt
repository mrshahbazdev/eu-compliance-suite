Directive (EU) 2022/2557 — Critical Entities Resilience. Sectors taxonomy, essential-services register, asset / site / dependency map, Art. 12 risk assessment, Art. 13 measures, Art. 15 incident reporting (24h / 1m).

== Description ==

Free tier covers Articles 12 / 13 / 15 / 17 with admin-side workflows. Significant-disruption incidents are tracked against the 24-hour early-warning + 1-month follow-up reporting deadlines.

= Coverage =

* **Annex sectors** — energy · transport · banking · FMI · health · drinking water · waste water · digital infrastructure · public administration · space · food.
* **Art. 12** — four-yearly risk assessment with overdue tracker.
* **Art. 13** — resilience measures across 8 categories (physical protection, continuity, recovery, access control, training, governance, supply chain, cyber).
* **Art. 15** — significant-disruption incident register with 24h / 30d deadlines.
* **Art. 17** — cross-border flag.

= Pro features =

* Live competent-authority submission (early warning + follow-up).
* Background-check workflow (Art. 14) — DBS / CRB / Führungszeugnis integration.
* Signed PDF reports + audit bundle.
* REST / webhooks for SIEM forwarding (Splunk · ELK · Datadog · Graylog).
* Cross-border critical-entity coordination (Art. 17) — multi-MS dashboards.
* Geographic-information map view (OpenStreetMap).
* WC essential-service per-product meta.
* WPML / Polylang.
* Multi-site network aggregator.
* 5,000-row CSV cap.
* Slack / Teams / PagerDuty alerts.
* NIS2 cross-link.
