# EuroComply — Brand & Positioning

## Name
**EuroComply**

## Tagline
> "EU compliance, one plugin at a time."

## Positioning
The affordable, honest, EU-made alternative to expensive compliance tooling (Cookiebot, OneTrust, UserWay, Quaderno). Built by Europeans, hosted in Europe, transparent about what it does and doesn't do.

## Target audience
- **Primary:** EU-27 solo merchants and SMBs running WooCommerce or Shopify who cannot afford a dedicated compliance team but fear fines and lawyer letters (Abmahnung in Germany).
- **Secondary:** Digital agencies serving EU SMBs, looking for a white-label compliance stack.
- **Tertiary (Phase 3+):** Mid-market EU e-commerce (€5M+ GMV) needing enterprise compliance (e-invoicing, DSA, EPR).

## Core values
1. **Honest compliance, not overlays.** No "AI accessibility widget" snake oil. Real fixes.
2. **EU-first.** EU-hosted (OVH/Hetzner/AWS Frankfurt). EU DPA. EU-27 coverage from day 1.
3. **Affordable.** Freemium on WordPress.org → paid Pro from €9/mo. Enterprise only when justified.
4. **Transparent.** Legal templates open-source. Pricing on website. DPA template public.

## Visual identity (MVP)
- Wordmark: `EuroComply` in Inter Bold
- Primary color: `#1F4E78` (EU-blue navy)
- Accent: `#FFD966` (EU-yellow gold)
- Icon direction: shield + EU 12-star motif
