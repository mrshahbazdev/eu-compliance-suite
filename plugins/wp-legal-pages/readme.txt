Generate EU-compliant legal pages (Impressum, Datenschutzerklärung, AGB, Widerrufsbelehrung) for WordPress and WooCommerce — DE, AT, CH, EU-27.

== Description ==

**EuroComply — Legal Pages** helps you meet the legal-page requirements that every EU commercial website needs, without paying €40/month to a legal-template service or risking an "Abmahnung" (cease-and-desist letter).

Fill in your business details once. The plugin generates:

* **Impressum** — required in Germany (§5 DDG / §5 TMG), Austria (§25 MedienG / §14 UGB), Switzerland (Art. 3 UWG)
* **Datenschutzerklärung (privacy policy)** — required under GDPR Art. 12–14 for every EU website
* **AGB (general terms)** — template for e-commerce (Pro)
* **Widerrufsbelehrung (withdrawal notice)** — required for EU B2C distance sales (Pro)

The plugin creates real WordPress pages, adds a footer-menu link helper, and provides shortcodes so you can embed the content anywhere.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin ZIP via **Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin**, or install from the WordPress.org plugin directory.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Go to **EuroComply → Legal Pages** and fill in your business information.
4. Click **Generate & Publish** — the plugin creates the pages and adds them to your footer.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this replace a real lawyer? =

No. EuroComply gives you legally-informed starting templates. For high-risk businesses (finance, healthcare, regulated products), we recommend you still have a lawyer review your pages.

= Is the Impressum content legally binding? =

You are responsible for the accuracy of the information you enter. EuroComply generates the standard structure required by law but cannot verify your registry numbers, addresses, or representative names.

= Can I translate the pages? =

Free tier supports German, English, and French. Pro tier supports all 24 EU official languages.

= Does it work with WooCommerce? =

Yes. Pro tier adds checkout-page links, order-confirmation email footer links, and the German "Button-Lösung" for Widerrufsbelehrung placement.
