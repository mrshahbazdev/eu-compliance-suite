# EuroComply — 18-Month Roadmap

**Vision:** An "EU Compliance Suite" brand shipping 18 plugins across WooCommerce, Shopify, WordPress, Chrome, Figma, and VS Code to cover EU-specific compliance end-to-end.

**Year-1 target:** 4,500 paying customers · €130k MRR · 10-person team · Series A-ready by M18.

## Phase overview

| Phase | Months | Theme | Plugins | Goal |
|-------|--------|-------|---------|------|
| 1 | M1–M3 | Cashflow foundation | Legal Pages · Cookie+GCM v2 · VAT/OSS | €2–5k MRR · solo dev |
| 2 | M4–M8 | Compliance heavy-hitters | GPSR · EPR · EAA | €15–30k MRR · team of 5 |
| 3 | M9–M14 | Flagship & enterprise | E-Invoicing · DSA · SEPA · Shipping | €80k+ MRR · pre-seed closed |

## Phase 1 plugins (current focus)

### 1. Legal Pages (`wp-legal-pages`) — WP + WC
- Impressum, AGB, Datenschutz, Widerrufsbelehrung
- EU-27 country templates (DE/AT/CH free; rest Pro)
- Auto-update subscription
- **Pricing:** Free + €9/mo Pro

### 2. Cookie Consent + Google Consent Mode v2 (`wp-cookie-consent`) — WP
- Auto cookie scan, GCM v2, IAB TCF 2.2
- Immutable consent audit log (DPA-ready)
- **Pricing:** Free + €9–29/mo Pro

### 3. EU VAT Validator + OSS Engine (`wp-eu-vat`) — WC
- VIES validation, OSS rates, IOSS, MOSS
- Quarterly filing prep
- **Pricing:** €19–49/mo
